Design Seoul 3rd Street: Apgujeong-ro Basic & Implementation Design.
Redefining the urban landscape through color, nature, and technology.

Location Gangnam-gu, Seoul
Role Landscape, 3D, Color
Year 2010

Urban Harmony through Color & Nature.

This project focuses on the 'Basic and Implementation Design' for the Apgujeong-ro 3rd Design Seoul Street. I participated as a key specialist, bridging the gap between planning and reality through detailed **Landscape Planning**, realistic **3D Visualization**, and strategic **Color Planning** to enhance the streetscape's identity.

Landscape Planning

Designing eco-friendly streetscapes that harmonize urban structures with nature.

3D Implementation

Creating high-fidelity 3D simulations to verify design feasibility and aesthetics.

Colorist

Applying a cohesive color palette that reflects the local identity and enhances visual comfort.
